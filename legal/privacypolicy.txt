Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2026

Audiobook Hub (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application and related services (the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy should be read alongside our Terms of Use and End User License Agreement (EULA).

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Audiobook Hub and covers information processed through:

our mobile application  
related features and functionality  
communications with us  

These are collectively referred to as the “Services.”

This policy does not apply to third-party services you access through the app.

2. Information We Collect

Audiobook Hub is designed to minimize data collection.

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect limited information you choose to provide, such as:

email address (if you contact support)  
support requests or feedback  
any information you include in communications  

Account creation may be required for certain features such as premium syncing or cloud-related functionality.

2.2 Information Processed Automatically

We process limited technical data necessary to operate core features such as:

subscription validation  
premium entitlement syncing  
device linking or transfer coordination  
service security and integrity  

This may include:

anonymous identifiers  
purchase and entitlement status  
basic device, app version, or session information  

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment card details.

2.3 Analytics and Diagnostics

Audiobook Hub uses analytics and diagnostic tools to improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

This may include:

usage events (for example, playback actions or feature usage)  
crash reports and error diagnostics  
performance metrics  

These tools may be provided by third-party services such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics.

Data collected through these services is used for app improvement, debugging, and service reliability, and is not used to personally identify you.

2.4 Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob.

These services may use device identifiers, including the Google Advertising ID, to:

display ads  
measure ad performance  
limit repetitive ads  

Advertising providers operate under their own privacy policies and may collect or process data independently.

2.5 Content and External Sources

Audiobook Hub allows you to access content from external sources such as:

RSS feeds  
podcast directories  
Google Drive or other user-connected cloud storage  
local files and folders selected by you  
torrent or magnet links (if configured by you)  

We do not collect, store, or monitor the content you access, except temporarily on your device as required for playback or transfers.

2.6 Peer-to-Peer (Torrent) Features

If you use torrent-based features:

your IP address may be visible to other peers  
your device may upload and download data  

Audiobook Hub does not track or log your file activity.

2.7 Payments and Purchases

Audiobook Hub may offer paid features such as subscriptions.

All payments are processed by third-party platforms (for example, Google Play).

We do not collect or store:

credit card details  
billing information  
payment credentials  

We may receive limited purchase-related data such as:

product identifiers  
purchase tokens  
subscription or entitlement status  

This is used only to verify purchases and enable premium features.

2.8 Third-Party Services

We may receive limited data from third-party services, including:

Google Play (billing and purchase validation)  
Firebase services (analytics and crash reporting)  
advertising providers  

These services operate under their own privacy policies.
