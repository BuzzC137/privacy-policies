2. Information We Collect

Audiobook Hub is designed to minimize data collection.

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect limited information that you choose to provide, such as:

any information you voluntarily include in communications

We do not require account creation to use the app.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Audiobook Hub does not intentionally collect or store personal data on external servers.

However, limited technical information may be processed locally on your device or through system-level services, such as:

We do not use this information to identify individual users. 

Audiobook Hub does not track users across apps, services, or websites and does not use analytics or advertising identifiers unless explicitly stated in future updates.

Audiobook Hub allows you to access content from external sources, including:

RSS feeds

We do not collect, store, or monitor the content you access, except temporarily on your device as required for playback.

2.4 Peer-to-Peer (Torrent) Functionality

If you use torrent-based features:

your IP address may be visible to other peers in the network
your device may upload and download file data directly

Audiobook Hub does not track, store, or log your file activity.

2.5 Payments and Purchases

Audiobook Hub may offer paid features, including subscriptions or one-time purchases.

All payments are processed through third-party platforms such as Google Play.

We do not collect or store:

We may receive limited purchase-related information from the platform, such as:

This information is used only to enable and manage access to paid features within the app.

All payment and transaction data is handled by the platform provider (such as Google Play) in accordance with their own privacy policies. Audiobook Hub does not have access to full transaction details or personal billing information.

2.6 Third-Party Data

We may receive limited information from third-party services, such as:
